# Project Overview — Christ Fields

## About

Christ Fields is a Christian technology company in early development. The name comes from Proverbs 27:17 — "As iron sharpens iron, so one person sharpens another." The mission is to build tools and communities that genuinely serve people rather than exploit or distract them.

The company sits at the intersection of faith, technology, and human development. Every product under the Christ Fields name is designed to help people grow in faithfulness, think clearly, and live with integrity — not just perform productivity.

---

## The Website

**URL:** [christfields2717.com](https://christfields2717.com)
**Type:** Static marketing and waitlist site
**Hosting:** Netlify (static deploy)

The site introduces the Christ Fields brand, presents the three projects currently in development, and collects early-access signups via a Netlify Forms waitlist. It is not a web application — there is no backend, database, or user authentication. The attack surface is intentionally minimal.

## The Ecosystem

### ScholarFlow *(Coming Soon)*

A student productivity and personal growth platform combining:
- AI-assisted task management
- Google Calendar / Google Docs integration
- Habit building and accountability tracking
- Bible study tools
- Personal wellness tracking

The goal is holistic development — academic, personal, and spiritual — rather than just productivity metrics.

### FaithFlow *(Coming Soon)*

Real in-person small groups meeting weekly. Structured around honest accountability, genuine friendship, and scripture. Not an app — a community. Brotherhood and sisterhood, face to face.

### OSINT & Trace *(In Development)*

Using open-source intelligence (OSINT) and cybersecurity skills to help locate missing persons. Technical work done in collaboration with organizations working toward the same goal. Faith expressed through work.

## Design Philosophy

- **Dark, minimal aesthetic** — Dark green-black background, gold accents, serif display typography (Cormorant Garamond), clean body text (Inter)
- **No frameworks** — Vanilla HTML, CSS, and JavaScript only. No React, no Tailwind, no build tools. Keeps the site fast and auditable.
- **Security-first from the start** — Headers, CSP, and form hardening implemented during initial development rather than as an afterthought
- **Honest copywriting** — Projects are labeled "Coming Soon" or "In Development" with no overpromising
