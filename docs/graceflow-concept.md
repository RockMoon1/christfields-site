# GraceFlow - concept directions (for you to choose)

GraceFlow isn't defined or built yet, and it's nowhere in the repo. Rather than
invent a product overnight, here are directions that fit the Christ Fields
"-Flow" pattern, where each product serves one life domain:

- **ScholarFlow** = productivity + discipline (the striving)
- **FaithFlow** = community + accountability (the togetherness)
- **GraceFlow** = ? (the theme of *grace* suggests rest, mercy, gratitude, or giving)

## Options

### 1. Grace toward yourself - rest, sabbath, anti-burnout  ⭐ (my pick)
A deliberate counterweight to ScholarFlow's discipline. Sabbath rhythms, rest
reminders, self-compassion rooted in grace rather than performance. Fits the
brand tension perfectly: discipline (ScholarFlow) balanced by grace (GraceFlow).
Reuses the **Receive a Word** verse engine for daily rest/gratitude prompts.

### 2. Gratitude + daily devotion
A grace/gratitude journal with daily devotionals, examen, and prayer journaling.
Lowest build cost - it's largely the verse/reflection engine we already built,
plus a journaling store. Could ship as the first GraceFlow MVP fast.

### 3. Generosity + giving
Habits of generosity: a giving/tithe tracker, acts-of-kindness streaks, serving
others. "Grace" as freely giving what was freely received.

### 4. Forgiveness + reconciliation
A guided, private practice for working through resentment toward forgiveness and,
where wise, reconciliation. Sensitive and high-value, but the heaviest to design
responsibly.

## Recommendation
**Option 1 or 2**, because they (a) complete the brand story (discipline + grace),
and (b) reuse the AI verse engine we already built and verified, so the first
version is cheap and fast on the $0 stack.
